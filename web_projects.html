Web Projects

Sessions

Paris By Night Cocktails

The user can add a new cocktail, giving it a name and upload a picture. They can also edit it, write a review or delete the ingredients.

Fresh Butler

A client can sign up and hire a butler through a booking system. Also, a butler can choose to accept or decline the request.
